How to make the Dish

Whipped eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter

Steps for the Preperation

  1. Whip the eggs with a spoon in a seperate bowl
  2. Warm up the butter and some oil in the pan at mid-level heat
  3. Let the eggs cook in the pan till they are in no more liquid like state anymore.
