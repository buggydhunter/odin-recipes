Boiled Potatoes with gracious Salt :)

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Cold Water

Steps for the Preperation

  1. Peal the Potatoes
  2. Fill a pod with just enough water that they are covered
  3. Let them boil under full heat
  4. Once it started boiling wait for an additional 20 minutes e viola enjoy :)
